## What the data represents

Natural Earth describes this as a cartographically selected set of city and
town points. It includes all admin-0 capitals and selected other cities.
Regional significance and map usefulness influence inclusion; it is not a
complete or strictly population-ranked list of world settlements.

Population fields are estimates intended for cartographic use. They should
not be represented as current official census counts.
